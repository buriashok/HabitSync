# Privacy Policy for HabitSync

**Effective Date: October 26, 2023**

HabitSync ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information
When you use HabitSync, we may collect certain personal information that can be used to identify you, including:
*   **Account Information:** If you sign in using Google Sign-In or Firebase Auth, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
*   **Contact Information:** Any information you provide if you contact us for support.

### b. Usage and Data
*   **Habit Data:** We collect and store the habits you create, your progress, streaks, and XP to provide the core functionality of the App. This data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore.
*   **Local Data:** We use Android DataStore to store local preferences and app settings on your device.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
*   Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
*   Sync your habit data across devices via your account.
*   Personalize your experience (e.g., displaying your name and profile picture).
*   Communicate with you regarding updates or support.

## 3. Third-Party Services
HabitSync uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:
*   **Google Play Services:** Used for app functionality and updates.
*   **Firebase Authentication:** Used for secure user login and account management.
*   **Firebase Firestore:** Used for cloud storage of your habit data.
*   **Google Sign-In:** Used to provide a convenient login method.
